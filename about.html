About our Team

At SafeDrive Driving School, we aim to help you achieve the biggest goal in life. We help adults and teens learn to drive safe and be effective. Our students come out of our school ready and prepared for their future. These goals are also shared with our staff as we will help fulfill your needs. Our staff is filled with experienced drivers and teachers. We are cooperative and patient with all our students as our goal is to help you. Not only will we teach you laws on the road, but we give our students one on one driving lessons. We understand the stress that may come during tests so we make sure to provide accurate feedback.
