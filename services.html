Welcome to SafeDrive Driving School!

You drive, We teach

All services require registration to a class in the past or present

Classes

Our classes help teach our students the laws and mechanics of driving. This one month course occurs two times a week where one of our wonderful lecturers will teach a 20 person classroom. This is a required to take if you wish to use any of our other services.



Practice & DOL Written Test

We provide practice written tests and the DOL written test to all our students. Our practice driving tests emulate exactly what you will see in a DOL written test. Our written tests provide you with a variety of questions you will see in the official test.

Simulation Driving

Our top of the line driving simulator replicates what you will see on the road in the classroom. It is great to understand the ways of the road before actually driving on them. It is also great to help learn maneuvers like parallel parking. Our simulator is as simple as turning on a computer and just like that, you are behind the wheel.

Observe Driving

Our students are able to sit in the backseat of one of our cars. You will be able to observe a fellow classmate or lecturer drive. This will help teach you how others react to certain situations. If a student is not driving, you will be able to get a lesson from the lecturer on the road.

Practice & DOL Driving Test

We provide practice driving tests and the DOL driving test to all our students. Our practice driving tests emulate exactly what you will see in a DOL driving test. Soon enough, you will be ready to take the official test with the lecturer you have driven hours with.

Tutoring

Our tutors are available to you for any help related to driving. All questions are accepted and do not have to do with the tests. Our tutors will help you make sure you know everything behind the wheel. We have plenty of tutors available in our building all you have to do is schedule an appointment with them.
